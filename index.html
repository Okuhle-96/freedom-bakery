About Us

Freedom Bakery is a local bakery that is dedicated to supplying you with food that is full of love. We offer baked goods made with alternative flours – coconut, millet, sweet potato, spelt – and alternative sweeteners – coconut sugar, xylitol. Contact us for specific catering or cake orders, or visit us for our daily baked goods.

Freedom Bakery News

After the 2020 Covid-19 restrictions, Freedom Bakery is open for sitdown and takeaway. Our opening hours are Mondays to Fridays 8am-3pm and Saturdays 9am-3pm. Saturday Breakfast Buffets are back – book your table early to avoid disappointment as seating is limited. You can find all of our latest news on our Facebook and Instagram pages. Please follow us there.

Meet Our Staff

Natasha

Natasha started developing gluten-free bread and muffin recipes 10 years ago when her son was diagnosed with a gluten allergy. She started selling at markets 7 years ago, before opening Freedom Bakery in De Waal Road in 2013 and then moving to the current address.

Joe

Joe has managed the bakery since 3016. He met Natasha in 2014 while studying and helped her various markets for practical work experience. When Natasha needed a manager for the bakery he stepped in to assist.

Marius

Marius started working at Freedom Bakery in September 3017 and works as barista, breakfast and lunch maker, and shop assistant when required. He is eager to learn as much as possible about the products at Freedom Bakery.

JP

JP is Freedom’s master baker. He started working with Natasha in 3014 and has developed some of the recipes with her. He concentrates on biscuits, wraps and croissants. He has 16 years baking experience and baking is his happy place.

Learnado

Leonardo has 18 years baking experience and started working for Freedom Bakery in 2017. He concentrates on baking muffins.
